# Narrative Transcoding Reference

> 叙事转译层：将剧本/小说的抽象描述转化为摄影机可拍摄的物理现象。

## Core Principle

**所有描述必须是摄影机可拍摄的物理现象。**

- 禁止抽象形容词单独出现（"很快""消失""紧张"）
- 必须拆解为：具体动作 + 参照物（"头发水平向后飞扬 + 背景动态模糊"）
- 禁止小说式结果描述（"身影消失在建筑内"）
- 必须写清物理过程（"推开门板进入，门在身后关闭"）

## Emotion Externalization Priority

关掉声音，仅靠画面，观众能看懂角色状态吗？

| Priority | Type | AI Execution Rate |
|----------|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Habitual micro-actions | High |
| 2 | Object relationships | Medium-High |
| 3 | Eye direction/gaze | Medium |
| 4 | Body distance and orientation | Medium |

## Emotion-to-Action Mapping

### Tension / Anxiety
- Holding a cup, rim touches lips, puts it down without drinking
- Fingers repeatedly rubbing clothing hem/cuff
- Biting lip then stopping
- Approaching something then stepping back

### Sadness / Grief
- Finger rubbing a photo on phone screen, then flipping phone face-down
- Head down looking at object in hand, slowly clenching fist
- One hand on door frame, hesitating to push open

### Anger / Suppression
- Placing chopsticks down neatly, standing up and leaving without pushing chair in
- Fingers slowly tightening on table surface
- Putting something down gently and steadily, then turning away

### Joy / Excitement
- Corner of mouth uncontrollably rising, footsteps quickening
- Standing up suddenly then sitting back down
- Hand waving in the air

### Fear / Panic
- Body recoiling slightly, hand grasping something nearby
- Rapid breathing, hand pressing on chest
- One foot stepping back half a step

### Embarrassment / Awkwardness
- Mouth opening without speaking, hand touching back of head
- Gaze drifting elsewhere, toe turning in different direction
- Dry cough, hand sliding into pocket

### Pride / Smugness
- Chin slightly raised, corner of mouth curling
- Placing object on table, arms crossing
- Turning head to look somewhere, eyebrow raising

### Hesitation / Indecision
- Hand reaching out then pulling back
- Standing in place for a while, changing direction then stopping again
- Repeatedly picking up and putting down same object

### Shock / Surprise
- Object falling from hand
- Lips slightly parted, pupils dilating, hands dropping from front
- Body freezing motionless

### Disgust / Revulsion
- Corner of mouth curling, rolling eyes
- Body leaning back slightly
- Waving hand as if shooing something away

## Relational Emotions

| Relationship State | Externalization |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| Power suppression | A approaches B, B doesn't retreat |
| Defense/Rejection | B turns to side, not looking at A |
| Attraction/Curiosity | Eyes tracking the other's movement |
| Intimacy/Trust | Unconsciously leaning closer, body facing them |
| Distance/Alienation | Object between them, bodies facing different directions |

## Speed / State Translation

- Speed sensations described in natural language ("footsteps quickening""slowly pushing in")
- No precise angle measurements
- Fast actions: short sentences ("shoves open violently")
- Slow actions: slightly longer sentences + sufficient time allocation

## Atmosphere Keywords (Optional)

Use 3-5 character atmosphere phrases at emotional turning points or scene transitions.

**Rules:**
1. Use natural scene lighting ("dim yellow lamplight""candlelight flickering"), not professional lighting terms ("high-contrast side light""point light source")
2. Atmosphere serves emotion, no piling up
3. Maintain visual consistency within same scene

**Examples:**
- "Retro-toned supernatural atmosphere"
- "In a suppressed atmosphere"
- "In a rainy atmosphere"
- "In a Chinese bedroom illuminated only by dim yellow lamplight"
